Biography

Dr. James P. Howard, II, known as the Count of Krigsvold and Baron of Matikonis, stands at the forefront of environmental conservation and scientific research within the unique ecosystem of Western Antarctica. With an extensive background in data science, mathematics, and environmental policy, the Count combines a passion for sustainable development and climate change advocacy with a commitment to preserving one of Earth's most vulnerable regions.

As the Count of Krigsvold, Dr. Howard leverages his expertise to further the mission of Westarctica. Through collaborative efforts with international organizations, public engagement, and spearheading innovative conservation projects, the Count aims to mitigate the impacts of climate change and promote the preservation of the West Antarctic Ice Sheet.

The Count of Krigsvold encourages public involvement in environmental conservation. Whether through supporting Westarctica's initiatives, participating in educational programs, or adopting sustainable practices, everyone has a role to play in combating climate change and protecting our planet's future.
